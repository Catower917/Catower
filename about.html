About Catower

Welcome to Catower, a security study group dedicated to exploring the latest trends and technologies in cybersecurity. Our group is made up of passionate individuals who are always looking to expand their knowledge and share their experiences with others.

Whether you're a beginner or an expert, we have something for everyone. Our meetings cover a wide range of topics, from basic security principles to advanced threat analysis techniques. We also organize workshops and events throughout the year to help members stay up-to-date on the latest industry developments.

If you're interested in joining Catower, we'd love to hear from you. Please reach out to us through our GitHub page or attend one of our upcoming events.
