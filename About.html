About Catower

Catower is a security study group that focuses on learning and discussing various topics in computer security. Our group was founded in 2019 and is composed of undergraduate and graduate students from various universities in Korea.

We hold regular meetings to discuss current events, conduct technical workshops, and work on various security projects. Our goal is to share knowledge and promote the growth of security professionals in Korea.

For more information about our group, please feel free to contact us at catower@gmail.com.
